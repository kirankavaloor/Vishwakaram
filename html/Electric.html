Electric Service

Batteries

Three of the main things you might think of in the electrical system in your car might be the battery, alternator, and starter. But there are other things that are included or impacted by these main systems. Here are some of the things that make up the electrical system in your car and are included in electrical service:

  • Starter
  • Alternator
  • Battery
  • Power windows
  • Power steering
  • Windshield wipers
  • Defroster
  • Media Player
