Our Services

General Service

Generally includes: Engine oil and oil filter change. Air and fuel filter check. General inspection (fluids, belts, hoses and brakes) Tyre check and rotation.

Modifying

Any modification that changes the look of and performance of any vehicle has to be assessed by the insurance provider. Engine changes, sports seats, body kits, a spoiler etc.

Electric Work

It's important to test your battery and electrical system regularly, not just when it's starting to show signs of weakness. Proactively testing it twice a year will help reduce your chances of failure. Most retailers offer a simple free five minute battery test.

Alignment

It is recommended to get your wheels aligned every 2 to 3 years. However, to ensure the optimal safety of your car, yourself and others around you, it is best to opt for a wheel alignment every time you go to change your oil of the vehicle.

Painting

The Denting process removes visible deformities on the exterior body of the car. In case of accidents, collision of vehicle denting and painting of the parts are preferred as it helps save the additional cost of the new part used.

Washing

A car wash (also written as "carwash") or auto wash is a facility used to clean the exterior and, in some cases, the interior of motor vehicles.
